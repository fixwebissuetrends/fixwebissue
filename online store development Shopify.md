Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is Shopify online store development?
Shopify online store development involves designing, customizing, and optimizing a Shopify store to create a high-performing eCommerce website tailored to business needs.
2. How long does it take to develop a Shopify store?
A basic Shopify store can take 7–10 days, while custom Shopify development may take 3–6 weeks depending on features and complexity.
3. Is Shopify good for small and large businesses?
Yes, Shopify is suitable for startups, small businesses, and enterprise-level brands due to its scalability and flexibility.
4. Can I customize Shopify beyond themes?
Absolutely. Shopify allows custom development using Liquid, custom apps, APIs, and Shopify Functions.
5. Is Shopify SEO-friendly?
Yes, Shopify provides built-in SEO features such as clean URLs, meta tags, fast loading speed, and mobile optimization.
